Sorcha's Art Page
The aim of this page is to show my Advanced Higher Art Portfolio from 2021
The theme of my portfolio was
REFLECTING ON LOCKDOWN
My theme was broken down into 2 parts, 'Reflection Imagery' and 'Lockdown Imagery' before coming together and Combining.
Reflection Imagery
This explored the medium of literal reflections of light and the concept of thinking back.
Lockdown Imagery
This looked into the key images of lockdown like masks and hand sanitiser.
'Reflecting' and 'Lockdown'
This combined the two earlier themes to showcase my theme of Reflecting on Lockdown.
